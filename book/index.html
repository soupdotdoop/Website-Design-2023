BATMAN:
The Black Mirror

Written by: Scott Snyder

Art by: Jock and Francesco Francavilla




Summary:

The plot tells the continued adventures of Dick Grayson (the former first Robin and Nightwing) as Batman, as Bruce Wayne continues his work for Batman Incorporated. Dick must battle against several villains, one of whom is selling various super villain weapons and items in an underground black market. Meanwhile, Jim Gordon and his daughter Barbara Gordon must contend with the return of James Gordon Jr., whom they have ex-communicated due to his continued psychopathy.


Genre: Superhero, mystery, thriller


Published: Janurary - October 2011


Rating: 9.5/10 (IGN), 4.3/10 (Goodreads), 4.7/5 (Amazon)
